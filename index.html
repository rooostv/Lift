AUTO-PROGRESSION RULES

App adds 2.5kg automatically next session. You log the kg you used — app does the math.

Mark session done, tap "Hard" on the lift. App keeps same weight next week.

RECOMP NOTES
Why accessories are 12–15 reps
Higher rep accessories burn more calories, build muscle at a deficit, and don't tax your CNS before main lifts. This is the recomp sweet spot.
Nutrition rule
Eat at a small deficit (200–300 kcal below maintenance). High protein — minimum 2g per kg bodyweight = ~192g/day. This preserves muscle while losing fat.
Strength may stall slightly
At a deficit, strength gains slow. That's normal and expected. The goal is recomp, not maximum strength gain. Stay patient — strength returns when you're leaner.
